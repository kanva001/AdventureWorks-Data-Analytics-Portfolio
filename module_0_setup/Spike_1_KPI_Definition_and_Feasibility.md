# Spike 1  KPI Definition & Feasibility

This spike establishes business-aligned KPIs for the AdventureWorks Inventory Accuracy & ICQA Analytics Program.

## Business Risk
Unclear or infeasible KPIs increase reconciliation effort and reduce confidence in reporting.

## Objective
Define ICQA-style KPIs that are meaningful, feasible, and reproducible.

## KPIs Approved
- Inventory On-Hand Quantity
- Inventory Variance
- Inventory Accuracy Percentage
- High-Risk SKU Identification

## Decisions
- KPI calculations documented upfront
- Assumptions explicitly stated
- Validation checks required per KPI

## Impact
Clear scope and reduced downstream refactoring risk.
